Who we are

Hi, I'm Torin — a filmmaker, fully in.

I started filming because I couldn't stand the thought of a day this big slipping into a handful of blurry phone clips. Thunder Bay raised me, and it's still home — but the best part of this work is that a good story will take you anywhere, and I'll happily go.

When you hire me, you get me. From the first email to the final frame, your day is mine to look after — nothing outsourced, nothing pre-packaged, no rotating crew you've never met. I shoot quietly and stay out of the way, so what ends up on screen is your day as it actually happened, not a version posed for the camera.

It's real. It's all in. And it lasts a lot longer than the flowers.
