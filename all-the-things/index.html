A note from Torin

Why the "All The Things" package had to exist.

As I write this, I've had the honour of delivering more than fifty wedding films. Each one has been different — different people, different places, different energy. But if there's one thing I've learned, it's this: some brides want everything.

Over the years, brides have asked me about filming their engagement session. Their bridal shower. A "day after" adventure shoot on a mountaintop. And every time, my answer was, "Yes. Yes. And absolutely yes." The truth is, I never had a clean, cohesive way to offer it all as one package. I never had a name for it. I never had a way to say, "You're that bride — and I built this just for you."

Until now.

The All The Things package exists for the bride who isn't just planning a wedding; she's looking to capture the entire experience. She wants to remember not just the big day, but everything that led up to it and everything that followed. It's for the bride who wants to hold on to the little moments: the way her fiancé laughed during their engagement shoot, the way her best friends toasted her at the shower, the wind catching her veil on that ridiculous cliff we hiked to the week after the wedding.

That said — this package isn't for everyone, and that's completely okay. I never want anyone to feel like they're "missing out" if it doesn't fit their vision, budget, or priorities. I still pour my heart into every single film I deliver. But if you've ever dreamed of going all in, of creating a wedding film that's more than just one day… this one's for you.

I created All The Things for the bride who feels like she's "too much" — because I think you deserve even more. Let's make something unforgettable.

— TorinYour videographer · director · honorary bridesmaid · professional cryer behind the camera

Everything you get when you go all in.

01 Engagement Video

Think engagement photos, but in motion. We'll film at the spot where you first met — or anywhere that means something to the two of you — and weave that footage through the rest of your deliverables. It's the opening chapter of your wedding day, told before the day even arrives.

02 Bridal Shower Coverage

Candid laughs, teary toasts, and heartfelt moments with your best girls — filmed like the celebration it truly is. We'll capture how it felt to be surrounded by the people who know you best, cheering you on before the big day.

03 Wedding Teaser Film

A high-energy, heart-tugging preview of your wedding day — edited and sent the next morning, while you're still recovering from tearing up the dance floor. You'll have editorial input on the song, the vibe, and a whole lot more.

04 Wedding Highlight Film

The crown jewel — a cinematic retelling of your entire day, carefully curated to capture every laugh, tear, dance move, and detail. This is the one you'll call your "wedding video," and the one you'll watch again, and again, and again.
