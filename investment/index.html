Wedding Cinematography Collections

I keep it simple. Rather than tiers built on hours, every wedding starts from the same foundation — what changes between collections is the set of edits you take home.

Every wedding includes:

  • Full-day coverage
  • A cinematic wedding film
  • A short social edit
  • Licensed music & full colour grade
  • Captured in 4K

Wedding Film

A cinematic wedding film that tells your story and carries the full emotion of the day. Vows and speeches are woven through a hand-picked score so the film can breathe and land on what matters most. Length varies by collection.

Wedding Short

A 20–40 second edit made for sharing. It captures the essence of your day in a distinct style — energetic, sentimental, or nostalgic — and is easy to revisit and easy to send to everyone who asks.

Documentary Edits

Straightforward, full-length edits of your ceremony and speeches. Multiple angles and fully-mastered audio preserve every word and moment in sight and sound — perfect for the parts of the day you'll want to relive in full.

Photography

Photography is available too, if you'd like stills alongside your film. Just mention it when you reach out and we'll work it into your day.
